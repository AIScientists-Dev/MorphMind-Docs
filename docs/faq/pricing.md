## Programs for Researchers & Students

We offer two programs to help researchers and students access AgentLab:

### Student Researcher Fellowship

For students at any level—high school through PhD—who want to explore AI research through agent development.

**Benefits:**
- Lifetime Pro account (valued up to $10,000)
- Technical support and cloud infrastructure setup
- Feature opportunities across MorphMind channels

[Apply for the Student Fellowship →](https://morphmind.ai/community/student-fellowship)

---

### Scholar Program

An invitation-based program for faculty, research professionals, and managers.

**Benefits:**
- Up to $50,000 in compute and research credits
- Dedicated collaboration with MorphMind engineering
- Cloud infrastructure and specialized model development support
- Full technical assistance at no cost

**No proposal required. No obligations.** Keep your agents private or publish research—your choice.

[Apply for the Scholar Program →](https://morphmind.ai/community/scholar-program)
