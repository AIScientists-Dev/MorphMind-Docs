# What is an AI Agent?

An AI agent is an intelligent system that can understand your goals, make decisions, take actions, and learn from experience—much like a capable research assistant who works alongside you.

Think of it this way: a regular chatbot answers your questions one at a time. An AI agent, on the other hand, can take on a whole project. It breaks down complex tasks, uses the right tools, follows your workflow, and keeps learning as it goes.

## Why AI Agents Matter

Existing AI solutions have real limitations:

- **They forget everything** — Each conversation starts from scratch
- **They don't know your field** — Generic models struggle with specialized work
- **They make things up** — Sometimes they sound confident but are wrong
- **They can't do multi-step work** — They're built for quick Q&A, not complex projects

AI agents are designed to solve these problems.

## What Makes AI Agents Different

### They Remember

Agents keep track of your projects, preferences, and past decisions. You don't have to re-explain everything each time.

### They Work Through Complex Tasks

Instead of just answering one question, agents can:
- Break big projects into smaller steps
- Run long processes from start to finish
- Connect to your tools, data, and workflows
- Adjust their approach based on what they find

### They Show Their Work

You can see exactly what the agent did and why. When it's unsure, it asks you instead of guessing.

### They Fit Your Domain

Agents can be customized with the knowledge, tools, and workflows specific to your field—whether that's biomedical research, drug discovery, data science, or education.
